Understanding Customer Feelings Through Yelp Reviews.

Value added

By understanding customer feelings through their reviews, the restaurant can make better decisions that lead to happier customers. This approach helps them stay competitive, bring in more repeat customers, and increase sales.

Objective

A local restaurant wants to make customers happier and improve its menu by learning what people think about their dining experience.

How it works

By using a technique called sentiment analysis, the restaurant can read and understand reviews left by customers on Yelp. This helps them figure out whether people are happy, unhappy, or neutral about their experience.

Steps involved

Data Collection

Airen Analytics will assist the restaurant in collecting Yelp review data, ensuring they have a comprehensive set of customer feedback to analyze.

Analyzing Sentiment

The reviews are then examined to see if they express positive feelings (like enjoying the food), negative feelings (like being unhappy with the service), or neutral feelings. This process helps identify what customers like and dislike.

Finding Insights

From the analysis, the restaurant discovers key themes, such as:

  • Customers love the atmosphere and friendly service but often mention being unhappy with certain dishes.
  • Some reviews highlight long wait times as an issue.
Taking action

With these insights, the restaurant can:

  • Improve popular dishes or introduce new ones based on what customers want.
  • Train staff to provide better service.
  • Promote their strengths, like the great atmosphere.
